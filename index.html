FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from local business owners before getting started.

How quickly will I see results from digital marketing? +
Google Ads and Instagram Ads can start generating leads within the first 3–7 days of launch. SEO takes 2–3 months to show significant results. Most of our clients see a clear improvement in enquiries within the first 2–4 weeks.
What is the minimum budget required to start? +
Our management fee starts at ₹5,000/month. In addition, you'll need an ad budget (we recommend a minimum of ₹5,000–₹10,000/month for ads on Google or Instagram). We work with you to maximize every rupee spent.
Do you work with businesses outside Hyderabad? +
Yes! While we have deep expertise in the Hyderabad and Telangana market, we work with local businesses across India. Our strategies are tailored to your specific city and target audience.
Will I get reports and updates on my campaigns? +
Absolutely. We provide monthly reports showing your leads, calls, clicks, cost-per-lead, and ROI. Standard and Premium clients get bi-weekly or weekly calls to review performance and strategy.
Is there a contract or minimum commitment period? +
We recommend a 3-month commitment for best results (ads need time to optimize). However, we don't lock you into long-term contracts — you can pause or cancel with 30 days notice.
Do I need to have a website to start running ads? +
Not necessarily. We can run WhatsApp and call-based lead generation campaigns without a website. However, a good landing page significantly improves results — and we can build one for you as part of our Premium plan.
